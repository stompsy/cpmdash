Strategic Insights

Why This Matters

Geographic Intelligence: Engagement locations reveal where overdoses are happening and where CPM resources are being deployed. This guides staff positioning, naloxone placement, and partnership development with location-specific stakeholders.

Risk Hotspots: Fatal case concentrations in specific location types inform targeted prevention efforts and emergency response protocol adjustments. Public venues may require different approaches than residential settings.

Intervention Context: Narcan administration rates by location type reveal field conditions and accessibility patterns. This data helps optimize supply distribution and training for location-specific scenarios.

Action Items: Use this data to optimize patrol routes, establish partnerships with high-volume venues, and deploy prevention resources where they're most needed.
