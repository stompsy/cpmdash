Strategic Insights

Why This Matters

Treatment Linkage: The SUD referral success rate is a critical care coordination metric. It measures the program's effectiveness at connecting individuals with substance use disorder treatment services—the bridge from emergency response to long-term recovery.

Care Continuum: High referral rates indicate strong partnerships with treatment providers and effective follow-up protocols. Low rates signal barriers that need addressing—whether systemic, resource-based, or patient-specific.

Fatal Case Context: Tracking SUD referrals among fatal cases reveals whether individuals who died had been connected to treatment resources. This informs prevention strategies and highlights the urgency of rapid treatment access.

Source Performance: Understanding which referral sources produce the highest SUD linkage rates helps identify best practices and optimize referral pathways. Replicate what works, fix what doesn't.

Action Items: Use this data to strengthen treatment partnerships, reduce referral barriers, improve follow-up protocols, and advocate for expanded SUD treatment capacity.
