Why This Matters

Referral Pathways: High-volume sources indicate established partnerships. Diverse sources suggest broad community awareness and multiple entry points for intervention.

Fatal Cases: Understanding which referral channels encounter the most critical situations guides resource allocation and training priorities.

SUD Linkage: Successful treatment handoffs measure care coordination effectiveness. Higher rates indicate stronger recovery pathways.

Action Items: Use this data to strengthen high-volume partnerships, identify referral gaps, and improve treatment linkage protocols.
