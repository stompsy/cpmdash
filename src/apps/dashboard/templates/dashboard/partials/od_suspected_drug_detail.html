Strategic Insights

Why This Matters

Substance Tracking: Identifying which drugs are involved in overdoses reveals community-specific threats and emerging trends. This intelligence drives targeted prevention messaging and informs naloxone distribution strategies.

Fatal Outcomes: The fatal rate shows the lethality of substances in the community. Higher rates signal the need for enhanced public warnings and emergency response protocols.

Narcan Effectiveness: Tracking naloxone administration rates measures field response capabilities and helps identify situations where additional intervention strategies may be needed.

Action Items: Use this data to target education campaigns, adjust naloxone supply chains, and coordinate with law enforcement on emerging substance threats.
