Strategic Insights

Why This Matters

Housing Stability: Living situation data reveals vulnerability patterns and helps identify populations at highest risk. Unstable housing often correlates with worse outcomes and requires tailored intervention approaches.

Risk Assessment: Understanding where individuals experiencing overdoses are living (or not living) informs outreach strategies, resource deployment, and partnership priorities with housing providers.

Treatment Accessibility: SUD linkage success varies by housing stability. This data highlights barriers to care and opportunities for integrated housing + treatment approaches.

Action Items: Use this data to coordinate with housing services, develop targeted interventions for unstably housed populations, and advocate for housing-first recovery models.
