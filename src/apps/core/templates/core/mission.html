Why Community Paramedicine

Community Paramedicine bridges gaps in the healthcare system—especially for individuals facing behavioral health needs, substance use disorders, chronic conditions, and barriers to accessing care. Our team reduces preventable 911 calls and ED visits, provides post-overdose follow-up, and creates warm handoffs to treatment partners.

  • Field-based support and recovery pathways (including MOUD referrals).
  • Home and outreach visits that reduce stigma and increase acceptance of services.
  • Preserving frontline units for high-acuity emergencies through targeted follow-up and navigation.
